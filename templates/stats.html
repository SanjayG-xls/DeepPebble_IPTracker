Deep, Transparent Answers.

Trust is built on clarity. Here are the answers to your most pressing questions.

Our philosophy is "privacy-by-design." We use a hybrid inference model, meaning sensitive data and personal memories (especially in Yovedha.ai) are processed on your device whenever possible. Data sent to the cloud is anonymized, encrypted, and used only to improve your specific experience, never for third-party advertising. You have granular control to view and delete your data at any time.

We use a proprietary "Model-of-Models" (MoM) approach. Instead of one giant, costly model, we use a smart orchestrator that routes your request to the best, most efficient model for the job. This includes our custom-trained models (for Indian languages and context), fine-tuned open-source models (for general reasoning), and specialized models (for finance in Finny or logistics in Vaayu). This makes our system faster, cheaper, and more precise.

Yovedha builds a secure, local "knowledge graph" on your device. This isn't a recording; it's a map of interconnected concepts, entities, and memories. When you mention "that presentation I gave in Mumbai," it can link "presentation" to "Mumbai" and the date "Last March." This graph is encrypted and inaccessible to anyone but you, allowing Yovedha to have long-term context without compromising your privacy.

Our entire suite is currently in a free, invitation-only beta. We are focused on building a world-class product and community. We will introduce transparent pricing models in the future, which will likely include a generous free tier for personal use and pro tiers for advanced features and business use. We will *never* sell your personal data to offset costs.
